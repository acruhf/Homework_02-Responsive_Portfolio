About Me


Virginia born and raised, I've been a lifeguard, landscaper, paddleboat merchant, prep cook, and hazardous waste technician. I've been in a dozen or so bands as a guitarist, bassist, drummer, and/or singer, including my current self-led 4-piece called Ulmeyda. Right now I'm building and decorating cakes at local Richmond bakery Shyndigz and I'm learning web development to launch a new career while stuck in quarantine. Visual arts are new to me but I love building big beautiful websites.
